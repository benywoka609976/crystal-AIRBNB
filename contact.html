Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property?

Simply browse our listings, select your desired property, choose your dates, and click "Book Now". You can also contact us directly for assistance with complex bookings.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by property. Most properties offer free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. Please check the specific property's cancellation policy before booking.

Are the properties verified?

Yes! Every property on StayKenya is personally verified by our team to ensure it meets our standards for quality, safety, and accuracy.

Do you offer long-term stays?

Many of our properties offer discounted rates for long-term stays (30 days or more). Contact us for information about long-term booking options.
