About StayKenya

Discover our story and mission to showcase Kenya's unique accommodations

Our Story

StayKenya was born from a passion for showcasing the incredible diversity of accommodations across Kenya. Founded in 2023, our mission is to connect travelers with unique, authentic, and memorable places to stay that go beyond standard hotel experiences.

We believe that where you stay can transform your travel experience. That's why we personally verify each property to ensure it meets our standards for quality, comfort, and authenticity.

From beachfront villas in Diani to mountain cottages in the Aberdares, from city apartments in Nairobi to luxury tents in the Maasai Mara - we're dedicated to helping you find the perfect place for your Kenyan adventure.

Our Values

Quality Assurance

Every property is personally verified to ensure it meets our high standards for comfort, cleanliness, and authenticity.

Trust & Transparency

We believe in honest pricing and clear communication. What you see is what you get with StayKenya.

Passion for Kenya

We're passionate about showcasing the best of Kenya and supporting local property owners and communities.

Exceptional Support

Our dedicated team is available 24/7 to ensure your stay is perfect from booking to checkout.
